Husqvarna

Hard Cross LE

Complete freedom of movement and reliable transport for traveling any distance

The Hard Cross LE is Husqvarna's new e-bike designed for city and off-road riding. It has a powerful engine and a capacious battery, thanks to which it can travel up to 180 km on a single charge, which is definitely higher than most competitors. And big wheels with pneumatic tires, disc brakes and an ergonomic seat make every ride much more enjoyable.

COMFORT AND SAFETY FROM HUSQVARNA

The Hard Cross LE electric bike will allow you to quickly and comfortably get to your place of work or study and not be tied to public transport. The bike has a solid construction and an impressive range, making it a reliable vehicle in today's urban environments. Lightweight and durable frame, 26-inch wheels and transmission system allow you to move confidently not only on the paved highway, but also on a difficult country track.

STRONG AND LIGHT FRAME

The frame of Hard Cross LE is made of durable and lightweight aluminum alloy. It is characterized by high reliability, stability in emergencies, excellent handling and comfortable movement on any road.

MODE COMBINATION FOR INCREASED RANGEÐ°

The Hard Cross LE e-bike has an extended range. Its 630 Ah battery with an intelligent energy optimization system allows you to drive up to 100 km on a single charge in electric drive mode. When using physical force on some sections of the path, the cruising range increases to 180 km. The maximum speed of the Hard Cross LE is 45 km/h.

RELIABLE TRANSMISSION

The transmission system provides smooth, precise shifting, comfortable speed control, and reliable operation. By shifting gears, you can drive up the hill with minimal effort and slide down safely.

ALL INFORMATION ON THE LCD DISPLAY

All the information on the LCD The large informative display, placed on the steering column, displays all the important information about the operation of the Hard Cross LE in real time. You can find out the current speed of movement, the charge level or the distance traveled. The display is protected from external factors according to the IPX7 standard and is not afraid of rain and dust.

COMFORTABLE SADDLE

The Hard Cross LE electric bike is equipped with a bicycle saddle from the well-known brand Selle Royal. The three-dimensional optimal structure of the gel saddle requires high resilience and high riding comfort.
